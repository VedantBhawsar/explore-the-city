People Also Ask About Pune


Which are the best adventure spots in Pune?

  1. Camping: Offering an escape from the monotonous escape from the city life, Kaas Plateau and Pawna lake are the most famous camping locations near Pune. The tents provided here are of the best quality and are fitted with all basic amenities.
  2. Trekking: Famous for their rocky trails and dotted with natural beauty throughout, places like the Rajmachi fort and Lohagarh fort offer the best terrains which are enough challenging for trekkers.
  3. Waterfall Rappelling: Many adventurous places in pune like Dudhiware Waterfall and Bekare waterfall offer you a chance to indulge in Waterfall rappelling. Here you can conquer the flow of water while going up and experiencing the huge flow hitting you.
  4. Paragliding: Soar above the mountains and experience the panoramic view while you glide above the lush valleys while enjoying a fun-filled session of Paragliding in Kamshet, Pune.

What are the famous adventure treks near Pune?

  1. Rajmachi Fort Trek: Standing on a height of 2759 feet, Rajmachi fort can be reached by trekking along the rocky terrains and steep slopes. Once you reach the top, the panoramic views of the Konkan Plains will offer you a sight to behold.
  2. Lohagad Fort Trek: Known to be one of the most scenic and picturesque Adventure places near pune, Lohagad Fort makes you trek through lush green surroundings and tranquil hidden landscapes to reach its ruins. The trek is easy and can be conquered by anyone with normal physique.
  3. Torna Fort Trek: Challenging the adventure seekers, a trek to this fort offers a thrilling yet beautiful experience. The track towards the top is filled with many rocky areas, and offers many panoramic sceneries from all sides.
  4. Kothaligad: Also known as the fort of Peths, this place was once used as a watch tower. Being placed on a height, Kothaligad offers a perfect trekking spot for the adventurous people and offers scenic environment to enjoy while conquering this fortress.
  5. Raireshwar Trek: Offering the best of nature as well as history, Raireshwar trek takes you towards the cloud while you traverse the multiple stairs and enjoy the serenity around you.

What are the best places for camping near Pune?

  1. Pawna Lake: Pawna Lake is considered to be the best spot for camping near Pune. Being one of the best adventure places in pune Pawna lake hosts around hundreds of people every day.
  2. Alibaug: Offering you a chance to camp around the beach, Alibaug hosts some of the best camping sites, each offering the best facilities available. In addition to the facilities, here you can also enjoy the live acoustic session while camping.
  3. Lonavala: A go-to destination for almost every resident of Pune, this hill station provides the visitors with the best climate, thus making it a perfect camping spot. The real essence of camping here can be felt as the moon shines in the sky and illuminates the scenic surroundings.
  4. Rajmachi Fort: Once a heritage building, this fort can be reached by trekking along the rocky trails and once you reach here, the mesmerising panoramic surroundings here offer a sight to behold. A session of camping at this spot lets you indulge in loads of fun around the blissful bonfire.
  5. Torna Fort: Standing amidst the surplus greens and serenity are the ruins of Torna Fort. Known to be a perfect spot for camping, many tourists come here to spend a night camping below the starry sky.
