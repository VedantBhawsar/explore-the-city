Bird Valley Udyan is situated in the heart of Pimpri-Chinchwad It stands as a tranquil retreat amidst the bustling city life. This spot is a haven for nature enthusiasts and family outings. We visited this park in last weekend to spent some relaxing time and a day out with family. This extensive park offers an array of attractions that promise a delightful day out. Let’s explore what makes this park a hidden gem in Pune.

Bird Valley Udyan Pune Address:

MR42+R37, MIDC, Chinchwad, Pimpri-Chinchwad, Maharashtra 411019 Bird Valley Udyan Pune Contact No: 022 6910 7600 Bird Valley Udyan Timing: 12 pm – 7 pm Daily (Open All days in Week) Bird Valley Udyan Entry Fee: Entry fee- Rs.10 Per person Kids above 5 years have same ticket charges. Bird Valley Udyan Parking Charges: For two-wheeler- Rs.5 For four-wheeler- Rs.10 Bird Valley Udyan Boating Charges: Motor boat- Rs. 50 Per person (for 1 round) Peddle boat- Rs. 50 Per person (For 1 round of 15 minutes) Laser show Timing: 7:30 Pm daily Laser Show Ticket Price: Rs. 20 per person
